Week 1 Learning Portfolio Artifact

Instructions

This week, you have met your teacher and classmates. You have found out some information about the college and the course. Also, you've had the chance to speak and listen in different activities. Perhaps you have had the chance to do something different.

Please answer the following questions thoughtfully. Take time, attention, and care with your answers.

1 What are some of the concepts, skills, and activities you have taken part in this week? (you can list these)
2 What did you enjoy? Why?
3 What did you find challenging? Why?
4 What did you find useful? Why?
5 What are you hoping to learn or develop on this course?
